Formats:

Case insensitice match:

s1 s2 s3 s4
d1 d2 d3 d4
site1 side2 side3 side4
disk1 disk2 disk3 disk4
sidea sideb sidec sided
diska diskb diskc diskd
Indices could be supported up to 9 (or 1 char).
One char allowed as separator:
s.1 s.2 s.3 s.4
d.1 d.2 d.3 d.4
side.1 side.2 side.3 side.4
disk.1 disk.2 disk.3 disk.4
side.a side.b side.c side.d
disk.a disk.b disk.c disk.d
_1 _2
_a _b
1 2
a b

Detection of such names could disable D64 extraction. E.g.
  inflating: /mnt/rd/lion2.ai64/ai64zip-102/Typhoon_S1.D64
